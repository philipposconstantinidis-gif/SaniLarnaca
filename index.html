Destinations

Where we are

Both properties are in prime locations with easy access to beaches, restaurants, and local attractions.

Sani Resort, Halkidiki

Our villa sits inside the renowned Sani Resort on the Kassandra Peninsula — one of Greece's most prestigious holiday destinations. Sani Resort offers pristine beaches, fine dining, a marina, and a nature reserve, all within a gated, eco-conscious complex. Halkidiki itself is famous for its crystal-clear waters, pine-covered hills, and warm Greek hospitality.

Thessaloniki Airport (SKG) is approximately 1 hour away by car.

Sani Beach Sani Marina Nature Reserve Fine Dining Watersports Kids' Clubs

Larnaca, Cyprus

Our apartment is in the vibrant coastal city of Larnaca — the gateway to Cyprus. With a historic old town, palm-tree-lined promenade (Finikoudes Beach), and easy access to the island's best attractions, Larnaca is the perfect base for exploring Cyprus. Stunning beaches, ancient ruins, and mountain villages are all within reach.

Larnaca International Airport (LCA) is just 15 minutes away.
