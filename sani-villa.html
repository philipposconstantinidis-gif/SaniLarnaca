Location

Larnaca is the gateway to Cyprus, with the island's main international airport just 15 minutes away. The apartment is well-positioned for exploring everything the city and island has to offer, with easy access to Finikoudes Beach (the famous palm-tree-lined promenade), the medieval castle, the old Turkish quarter of Skala, and dozens of restaurants and cafes.

Larnaca is also perfectly positioned for day trips: the Troodos Mountains are about 90 minutes away, Ayia Napa and Protaras beaches are 45 minutes east, and the capital Nicosia is 45 minutes north. Limassol and Paphos are easily reachable along the coast.

Things to Do in Larnaca

Larnaca has a rich history stretching back over 6,000 years. The city's highlights include the Church of Saint Lazarus (a stunning 9th-century stone church said to house the tomb of the biblical Lazarus), the medieval Larnaca Castle, and the Hala Sultan Tekke mosque on the banks of the Larnaca Salt Lake — a winter resting spot for thousands of flamingos.

For beach lovers, the McKenzie Beach area just south of the airport offers excellent seafood restaurants with views of planes landing overhead, while the quieter beaches of Pervolia and Mazotos are a short drive away. The famous Zenobia wreck — one of the world's top 10 dive sites — is accessible from Larnaca's fishing harbour.

In the evenings, Finikoudes promenade comes alive with bars, cafes, and street performers. For a more authentic experience, head to the backstreets of Skala for traditional Cypriot meze at family-run tavernas.
