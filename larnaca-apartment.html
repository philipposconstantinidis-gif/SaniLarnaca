Explore Larnaca

What you can do here

🏖️

Finikoudes Beach

The iconic palm-lined promenade with golden sand, beach bars, and cafes stretching along the waterfront.

🤿

Zenobia Wreck Dive

One of the world's top 10 dive sites — a sunken ferry accessible right from Larnaca's harbour.

🍽️

Cypriot Meze

Traditional tavernas in the old town of Skala serving endless plates of authentic Cypriot food.

🏛️

Church of St Lazarus

A stunning 9th-century stone church in the heart of Larnaca, said to house the tomb of Lazarus.

🦩

Salt Lake & Flamingos

Thousands of flamingos visit in winter. The Hala Sultan Tekke mosque sits on the lakeshore.

🏝️

Ayia Napa & Protaras

Cyprus's best beaches are just 45 minutes east — crystal coves, sea caves, and beach clubs.

⛰️

Troodos Mountains

Scenic mountain villages, hiking trails, and painted Byzantine churches. 90 minutes inland.

🌅

McKenzie Beach

Seafood restaurants with plane-spotting views as aircraft land over the beach. A Larnaca favourite.
